# Welcome to KBForge

This is your first KBForge article.

## How to use this article
1. **Edit this file** to document your first domain topic.
2. **Update the `review_due`** date.
3. **Run `python3 init.py`** (if you haven't) or manually run the catalog scripts in `01 Reference/scripts/` to index this file.

## What next?
- Move your real sources to `00 Raw Sources/`.
- Use the `triage` or `ingest-source` skills to process them.
- Add questions to `05 Iteration Logs/KB Gaps.md` when you find holes in your knowledge.
